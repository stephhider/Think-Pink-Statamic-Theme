This theme is based off of the baseline [theme](https://github.com/statamicthemes/statarkers-theme) put together by Garth Redfern. It uses the [UIkit framework](http://getuikit.com/index.html), [lightbox2](http://lokeshdhakar.com/projects/lightbox2/) and [owlcarousel](http://owlgraphic.com/owlcarousel/). The theme requires a Statamic license which is purchase-able from their [website](http://www.statamic.com/). Special thanks to Jason Varga for the [global addon](https://github.com/pixelfear/Statamic-Globals). It was designed by [Stephanie Hider](http://stephaniehider.com) in honor of her Grandmother who is a breast cancer survivor for 25 years now.
